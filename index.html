☀️ Seasonal Weather Advantage (Phu Quoc Island)
October marks the end of the rainy season in Phu Quoc. You can expect warm, tropical weather (25–30°C) with rapidly declining rainfall and clearing skies, making it an ideal beach destination. By substituting Central Vietnam (Da Nang/Hoi An) with Phu Quoc, you successfully avoid the peak monsoon and flooding risks in the central provinces, securing a sunny, relaxing resort holiday.

2. Destination Summary

Leg 1: Japan | 9 Nights

Period: Oct 9 – Oct 18

Highlights: Tokyo (Modern landmarks), Kyoto (Historic temples), & Osaka (Samurai castle & culinary scene)

  • Day 1 (Oct 9) Arrive Tokyo Haneda (HND) in evening, transfer to hotel in Shinjuku (e.g. Shinjuku Prince Hotel).
  • Day 2 (Oct 10) Explore Shibuya Crossing, Shibuya Sky, Meiji Jingu Shrine, Harajuku, and dinner at Omoide Yokocho.
  • Day 3 (Oct 11) Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, teamLab Planets in Toyosu, and sunset waterfront walk in Odaiba.
  • Day 4 (Oct 12) Shinkansen (Bullet Train) to Kyoto, check in to boutique hotel, and walk the lantern-lit lanes of Gion.
  • Day 5 (Oct 13) Beat the crowds at Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and Tenryu-ji Temple, followed by Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji).
  • Day 6 (Oct 14) Hike Fushimi Inari Shrine (vermilion torii gates) and take an afternoon trip to Nara Park (deer and Todai-ji).
  • Day 7 (Oct 15) Train to Osaka, check in to hotel (e.g. Cross Hotel Osaka), visit Osaka Castle and eat street food in Dotonbori.
  • Day 8 (Oct 16) Day trip to the historic Himeji Castle via Shinkansen OR spend the day at Universal Studios Japan.
  • Day 9 (Oct 17) Browse retro Shinsekai, shop in Shinsaibashi, and enjoy night views from Umeda Sky Building.

Leg 2: Vietnam | 10 Nights

Period: Oct 18 – Oct 28

Base: Phu Quoc Island Beach Resort (e.g., La Veranda Resort Phu Quoc - MGallery / Salinda Resort)

  • Day 10 (Oct 18) Flight KIX → SGN → PQC. Transfer to luxury beach resort, watch first tropical sunset on Long Beach.
  • Day 11 (Oct 19) Resort decompression: beachfront breakfast, infinity pool lounging, and premium spa treatments.
  • Day 12 (Oct 20) Island economy tour: visit traditional fish sauce factory (Nước Mắm), pepper farm, and taste Sim wine.
  • Day 13 (Oct 21) Day trip to the gorgeous Sao Beach (Bãi Sao) for powdery white sand, swimming, and beachside fresh seafood.
  • Day 14 (Oct 22) Take the record-breaking Hon Thom Oversea Cable Car to Pineapple Island and Sun World Nature Park.
  • Day 15 (Oct 23) At leisure: enjoy stand-up paddleboarding, kayaking, or reading under a palm tree.
  • Day 16 (Oct 24) Explore Ham Ninh Fishing Village on the east coast for local flower crabs, and hike to Tranh Stream waterfall.
  • Day 17 (Oct 25) Historical & cultural sights: visit Coconut Tree Prison museum and the coastal Ho Quoc Zen Pagoda.
  • Day 18 (Oct 26) Walk Mediterranean-style Sunset Town, view Kiss Bridge, and explore Duong Dong Night Market.
  • Day 19 (Oct 27) Final day of indulgence: beachside walks, sunset swim, and beachfront farewell dinner.
  • Day 20 (Oct 28) Packing and leisure. Mid-afternoon check-out and transfer to airport for overnight connection to Sydney via SGN.
