# 3-Week Japan & Phu Quoc Island Holiday Itinerary (October 2026)

## 0. Executive Summary

- **Duration:** 20 Days (3 Weeks)
- **Countries:** Japan (10 Days), Vietnam - Phu Quoc Island (10 Days)
- **Routing:**
  - SYD → HND (Tokyo Haneda) | Direct Flight (ANA/JAL/Qantas)
  - KIX (Osaka) → SGN (Ho Chi Minh City) → PQC (Phu Quoc) | 1-stop Connecting Flight (Vietnam Airlines)
  - PQC (Phu Quoc) → SGN (Ho Chi Minh City) → SYD (Sydney) | 1-stop Connecting Flight (Vietnam Airlines)
- **Map:** [Interactive City Routing Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=14uls7QPn8ESDw8Q6V40x5HKWhSHoYiA&usp=sharing)
- **Efficiency:** Optimized layovers at SGN hub (~2 hours) to avoid backtrack. Direct SYD-HND inbound.
- **Budget Estimate:** $20,802 AUD total for 2 travelers (Premium Mid-Range)

> [!TIP]
> **Seasonal Weather Advantage (October in Phu Quoc):** October marks the end of the rainy season in Phu Quoc. You can expect warm, tropical weather (25–30°C) with rapidly declining rainfall and clearing skies, making it an ideal beach destination. By substituting Central Vietnam (Da Nang/Hoi An) with Phu Quoc, you successfully avoid the peak monsoon and flooding risks in the central provinces, securing a sunny, relaxing resort holiday.

## 3. Destination Breakdown

### **Leg 1: Japan (10 Days / 9 Nights)**

_Focus: Traditional Culture, Modern Icons & Culinary Exploration_

- **Tokyo (3 Nights): Oct 9 – Oct 12**
  - **Day 1 (Oct 9):** Depart Sydney on an overnight flight, arriving at Tokyo Haneda (HND). Transfer to your hotel (e.g., _Shinjuku Prince Hotel_) via airport limousine bus. Grab a light local dinner in Shinjuku.
  - **Day 2 (Oct 10):** Walk across the famous Shibuya Crossing, ascend to the Shibuya Sky observation deck, stroll through the quiet forest of Meiji Jingu Shrine, and explore Harajuku. Conclude the evening with an izakaya crawl in Shinjuku’s Omoide Yokocho.
  - **Day 3 (Oct 11):** Visit Senso-ji Temple in the historic district of Asakusa. In the afternoon, experience the immersive digital artwork at teamLab Planets in Toyosu, followed by a sunset walk along Odaiba's waterfront looking over the Rainbow Bridge.

- **Kyoto (3 Nights): Oct 12 – Oct 15**
  - **Day 4 (Oct 12):** Board the Shinkansen (Bullet Train) from Tokyo to Kyoto (approx. 2h 15m). Check in to your boutique accommodation (e.g., _Kyoto Granbell Hotel_). Spend the afternoon wandering through the wooden lanes of Gion, searching for geishas.
  - **Day 5 (Oct 13):** Head to Arashiyama Bamboo Grove early morning to beat the crowds, followed by Tenryu-ji Temple. In the afternoon, visit Kinkaku-ji (The Golden Pavilion) to see its gold leaf reflect on the surrounding pond.
  - **Day 6 (Oct 14):** Hike the thousands of vermilion torii gates at Fushimi Inari Shrine. In the afternoon, take a 45-minute train ride to Nara Park to bow to the free-roaming deer and visit the giant Buddha at Todai-ji Temple.

- **Osaka (3 Nights): Oct 15 – Oct 18**
  - **Day 7 (Oct 15):** Take a short 30-minute train south to Osaka. Check in to your hotel (e.g., _Cross Hotel Osaka_). Explore the historic grounds of Osaka Castle, and head to Dotonbori at night for a street-food feast of Okonomiyaki and Takoyaki.
  - **Day 8 (Oct 16):** Take a Shinkansen to Himeji Castle (Japan's most pristine original samurai castle, 30m) OR spend a thrilling day at Universal Studios Japan.
  - **Day 9 (Oct 17):** Explore the retro Shinsekai district for a Kushikatsu lunch. Spend the afternoon shopping in Shinsaibashi and catch panoramic night views of the Osaka metropolis from the Umeda Sky Building.

---

### **Leg 2: Vietnam (10 Days / 9 Nights)**

_Focus: Tropical Beaches, Marine Activities & Boutique Luxury Resort Relaxation_

- **Phu Quoc Island (10 Nights): Oct 18 – Oct 28**
  - **Day 10 (Oct 18):** Check out of Osaka and take the flight from KIX to Phu Quoc via Ho Chi Minh City (SGN). Check in to your luxury beach resort (e.g., _La Veranda Resort Phu Quoc - MGallery_ or _Salinda Resort Phu Quoc_). Walk on the sand and watch your first tropical sunset.
  - **Day 11 (Oct 19):** A dedicated day of resort decompression. Sleep in, enjoy a beachfront tropical breakfast, lounge by the infinity pool, and book a traditional massage at the resort spa.
  - **Day 12 (Oct 20):** Learn about the local island economy. Visit a traditional fish sauce factory (Nước Mắm), tour a local pepper farm, and taste Sim wine at a local vineyard.
  - **Day 13 (Oct 21):** Spend a sun-soaked day at Sao Beach (Bãi Sao), famous for its powdery white sand, swaying palms, and clear turquoise waters. Feast on grilled seafood at a beachside shack.
  - **Day 14 (Oct 22):** Board the spectacular Hon Thom Cable Car (the world's longest oversea cable car) from the south of the island to Pineapple Island. Spend the day exploring nature and visiting the water park.
  - **Day 15 (Oct 23):** Completely unscheduled resort day. Enjoy the private beach amenities, try stand-up paddleboarding or kayaking, and read a book by the sea.
  - **Day 16 (Oct 24):** Discover the island's quieter eastern side. Visit the rustic Ham Ninh Fishing Village for a lunch of local flower crabs, and hike to the scenic Tranh Stream waterfall in the tropical forest.
  - **Day 17 (Oct 25):** Dive into the island's culture and history. Visit the historic Coconut Tree Prison (Phu Quoc Prison) museum, followed by a serene afternoon at the majestic Ho Quoc Pagoda Zen Monastery, which overlooks the sea.
  - **Day 18 (Oct 26):** Stroll through the Mediterranean-themed "Sunset Town" in the afternoon, view the Kiss Bridge, and spend the evening exploring Duong Dong Night Market for street food and local pearls.
  - **Day 19 (Oct 27):** Your final full day in paradise. Indulge in premium resort amenities, enjoy a sunset swim, and have a special beachfront farewell dinner.
  - **Day 20 (Oct 28):** Free morning for packing and beach walks. Mid-afternoon check-out and transfer to Phu Quoc International Airport (PQC). Board your overnight flight back to Sydney via SGN (arriving SYD on Oct 29).
